Credit score tips to help you build a healthy profile

By: Branden Secaira

Have you ever wondered what a credit score is or what exactly it means money wise? Look no further as we will explain it to you as simple as possible!

You should think of your credit score as your status or "state" of your bank account and how well it is percieved. You might ask, why is it so important and how can I get the best score? There are many ways for one to do this and for beginners, it starts with how you pay and take from your local bank. If you can remember these tips, you can take great care of you credit score!

People who can give you money such as car loan lenders or credit card issuers look at your credit score and depending on how high it is, it will depend on whether you are elligible for said loans or credit. If your credit score is not high enough, these people will not give you money or in this case, loans or credit until your credit score is high enough. You might ask, how do you increase your credit score?

Credit scores increase when you use are able to utilize credit less, low balances on credit cards but most importantly, paying on time! While there are other ways to increase your credit score, paying things such as bills or loan payments on time increases your credit score due to it showing that you are a great customer and that you are worthy of them giving you their money whenever you ask for it. Think of it as a trust between two friends.
