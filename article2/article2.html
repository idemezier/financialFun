Learn to protect your money from attacks

By: Branden Secaira

As our lives become more digital with technology becoming more advanced as we speak, there are ways criminals can get into your information with malicious methods such as phishing scams, weak passwords, card scanners and fraudulent texts. With all these in mind, you might be overwhelmed with the many methods people will take to steal you money, but don't fret! There are also many methods you can use to protect your money too!

To begin, you must use strong passwords for you account and if possible, 2 factor authentication. That way, if your password is found out (unless your have a really good password), they must go through another wall of security. For phishing scams and fraudulent texts/calls, beware of random links or any strange texts messages/calls you may receive out of nowhere. These may be an attempt at a hacker or a scammer trying to find a way to take money from you or to try and gain access to your device to take your information to take your money with that information. For card scanners, beware of people who may be getting too close to where you have your card, especially if you are leaving it unsupervised. You may also buy special card holders that block these scanners so they can be safe from those who attempt to sneakily scan them from you.
